--- layout: post title: 3:AM goes Brazilian date: '2007-05-29T00:00:00+00:00' permalink: "/3-am-goes-brazilian/" --- 3:AM Magazine, whose Edgier Waters anthology Snowbooks published last summer, has just launched 3:AM Brasil, a Portuguese-language version of the site dedicated to new writing, music and culture from Brazil. In these translation-poor times (the UK has one of the lowest proportions of literature in translation in the developed world), it's great to see such a promising new venue for cultural exchange. Go check it out - and if you are Brazilian, or know any Brazilians, spread the word.
