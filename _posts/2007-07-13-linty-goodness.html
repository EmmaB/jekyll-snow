Steve Aylett, the esteemed and brilliant author of LINT, is doing a reading/performance on July 22 at stand-up venue the Troy Club, CROBAR, 17 Manette Street, London W1D 4AS (just next to Foyles). It starts around 7.30pm. Other performers are Toby Hadoke, Liam Mullone, Eric's Tales Of The Sea and the MC is Andrew O'Neill. And you should go. If you buy a copy now, by the time the reading comes around you might have learned enough off by heart to read along with Steve. Who knows. I'm sure he'd love that.
