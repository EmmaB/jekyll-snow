Great news! George Mann's The Affinity Bridge is shortlisted for the ultra prestigious Sidewise Award. Here's the full list. The Affinity Bridge, by George Mann (Snowbooks/Tor, 2009) The Dragon's Nine Sons, by Chris Roberson (Solaris) Half a Crown, by Jo Walton (Tor) Nation, by Terry Pratchett (HarperCollins/Doubleday UK) Swiftly, by Adam Roberts (Gollancz) Fabulous company, no? Congratulations, George!
