--- layout: post title: Mad old world date: '2007-07-18T00:00:00+00:00' permalink: mad-old-world --- These fine fellows, 7 Inch Stitch, are a band who have named their first two releases after Jeff Lint books - 'I Blame Ferns' and 'I Eat Fog'. Jeff Lint, you'll recall, is the subject of 'Lint', Steve Aylett's latest book. Well, er, his latest novel. Yes, Jeff Lint is a ficticious character, whose books were therefore also fictitious, and now a real band has named their songs after them. And from their myspace page, it appears that Jeff Lint is also a member of the band. He must be very chuffed at how this is all working out...
