Slightly out of date, here's a post on Anti Plagiarism Day: ------------ I was sent a couple of stories by a good friend who alerted me to the fact that one of them has won a prize. Thing is, it was written after its author read the other story, which is pretty much identical in every way that matters - story, form, even horribly similar sounding character names. Plagiarism is dreadful. Read the comments on this post to see one author's reaction when she discovered her work had been plagiarised.
